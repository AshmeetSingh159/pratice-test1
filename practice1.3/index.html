Pre-ordered plants are sent out as close to the day quoted as possible, and typically we send all your items at the same time. Your plants are packed by our experienced nursery team to ensure they arrive in perfect condition to thrive in your garden.

You pay just one delivery charge no matter how big your order. The more you buy, the more you save!

Please note: It is difficult to give exact delivery times for plants as they are living beings so we sometimes have to wait until the are the right size to send to you. If very hot or very cold weather is forecast, we may delay sending your order because extremes of temperature could damage your plants. Delivery may also take slightly longer when there are bank holidays. For these reasons, please allow up to 14 days before you contact us.

Tracking your Order

Large orders are tracked and we'll send you an email to let you know your order is on its way with a tracking link tofollow. Please note that no signature is required. This is to avoid perishable items being stuck at depots and deteriorating, and it means you don't have to wait in for your goods to arrive. Your courier will leave your goods in a safe place - you can add a short message when you check out to give specific instructions.

Smaller, lighter, single-item orders are sent by Royal Mail without tracking.

Returns and Refunds

All our products are supplied with a complete and unconditional guarantee for the first 30 days. If you have any issues at all, simply get in touch with our Customer Service on cs@yougarden.com to arrange a return. Very occasionally plants are damaged in transit, and we'll do everything we can to put things right, We’ll pay return postage on any damaged products and send you a refund or replacement. If you change your mind, we'll give you a full refund - you just have to pay postage
